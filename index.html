Our Solution

PasarMIKRO is the first agricultural commodity trading platform for farmers, livestock breeders, fishermen, and micro traders in Indonesia, with many features tailored to their needs.

Mikro ERP

A lightweight ERP system that helps smallholders and traders manage inventory, orders, and payments in one simple interface.

Learn More

Traceability

Real-time tracking of products from farm to brand, ensuring compliance with sustainability and sourcing standards like EUDR.

Learn More

Trade Finance

Working capital solution based on verified transactions, enabling faster payments and reducing reliance on informal credit.

Learn More

Supplier Programs

A structured onboarding and support program that trains smallholders in digital tools, financial literacy, and ESG compliance.
