PasarMIKRO Solutions

PasarMIKRO presents four main pillars of solutions to support the transformation of the agricultural supply chain in Indonesia: Trade Finance, Farmer Empowerment Program, Product Traceability, and ERP Integration. Each solution is designed to empower farmers, aggregators, and processors with inclusive, transparent technology oriented toward environmental sustainability and global compliance.

Trade Finance (Talangin)

What It Is: Trade Finance or Talangin is a short-term working capital financing feature for traders, aggregators, and agricultural processors using PasarMIKRO.

Why It Matters: Many agricultural businesses experience cash flow gaps between purchasing harvests from farmers and receiving payments from buyers. Formal banking services are often inaccessible due to limited credit history and collateral.

How It Works:

  • Users transact through PasarMIKRO, automatically building a credit profile.
  • Credit limits are tailored based on validated transaction volume and frequency.
  • Repayment is done automatically through the platform's transaction settlement.

Impact: Accelerates payments to farmers, reduces reliance on informal financing, and enhances transparency in agricultural credit risk assessment.

Farmer Empowerment Program

What It Is: The Farmer Empowerment Platform (FEP) is an educational program that assists farmers, cooperatives, and micro-traders in accessing modern supply chains with adequate literacy.

Why It Matters: Many farmers do not understand pricing mechanisms, global ESG standards, or how to use digital technology for transparent record-keeping.

How It Works:

  • Farmers are connected to educational content via the app or local agents.
  • Content includes financial literacy, ESG sustainability standards, and digital tools usage.

Impact: Enhances farmers' capacity to meet global market demands, maintain crop quality, and support a transparent, sustainable farming system.

Product Traceability Tools

What It Is: PasarMIKRO’s traceability system combines location data, transaction logs, and satellite imagery to track product origin and environmental impact of farming activities.

Why It Matters: International regulations like the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) require evidence that commodities are not linked to deforestation or illegal land use.

How It Works:

  • Collects farmer location, transaction, and production capacity data.
  • Integrates with Google Earth Engine to monitor deforestation and land changes.
  • Estimates carbon credit based on land cover and biomass.

Impact: Supports global compliance, provides real-time ESG dashboards for buyers, and opens income opportunities through carbon credit schemes.

ERP Integration (Mikro ERP)

What It Is: Mikro ERP is a mobile-based inventory, payment, order, and financial record management system designed for small to mid-sized agriculture sectors.

Why It Matters: Conventional ERP systems are too complex and costly for small traders or farmers in rural areas with limited connectivity and digital literacy.

How It Works:

  • Manages inventory, payments, and orders in one simple system.
  • User-friendly interface supports offline usage.
  • Implemented in areas across Java, Sumatra, Bali, and Eastern Indonesia.

Impact: Reduces operational inefficiencies, accelerates transaction digitization, and enhances transparency and integration among farmers, traders, and buyers.
