Vision & Mission

PasarMIKRO is Determined to Empower #MikroHeroes

The Agri commodity market is vital and fascinating worldwide — because people will always eat! Today, large companies benefit significantly, while small farmers, suppliers, and buyers are left behind.

Indonesia's $130 billion agricultural market has been shaken since the pandemic. The fragmented and inefficient supply chain of smallholder ecosystems revealed its fragility during these hard times.

PasarMIKRO is a social enterprise determined to empower Agri #MikroHeroes through platform and digitization, to create a resilient and efficient people's agri-supply network. We've identified their most urgent problems and developed our solutions around them.
